The Best Salmon You'll Ever Eat

A perfectly cooked salmon filet.

Ingredients

  • 2 Wild Caught Salmon Filets
  • High Quality Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Dry salmon completely using paper towels.
  2. Generously season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the pan on medium high heat. Drizzle a small amont of oil in the pan.
  4. Place salmon skin side down and press onto pan for 20-30 seconds.
  5. Cook until almost completly done with the skin side down (4-6 mins). Flip for 20-30 seconds. Remove from heat. *Shoutout to Kenji for this technique.
  6. PSA to the world: Stop overcooking your god damn salmon. It's a beautiful fish that deserves to be enjoyed properly.
