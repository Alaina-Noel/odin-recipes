Quesadilla

A cheesy crispy quesadilla.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Chicken Breast
  • Trader Joe's Taco Seaoning
  • Full Fat Yogurt
  • Butter
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Tortilla
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Fresh Cilantro (Optional)

Steps

  1. Cook chicken in trader joe's seasoning. Chop chicken.
  2. Lightly butter a cast iron skillet & place tortilla on skillet.
  3. Sprinkle cheese onto tortilla and chicken.
  4. Fold in half & turn once until done.
  5. Serve with a side of yogurt and topped with fresh cilantro.
